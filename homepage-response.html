TestAutothon - A Hackathon Designed for Testers.

TestAutothon is the ultimate brain challenge for QA professionals—a live contest where teams are handed a problem statement, crack their way through it, and present their solution to the jury.

Every great tester knows the real thrill begins when the clock is ticking, the problem is complex, and the room is buzzing with energy—that’s the spirit of TestAutothon 2025.

In just three adrenaline-charged hours, your team will dive headfirst into a live automation challenge, racing against time to prove not only technical mastery but also the creativity, collaboration, and sharp thinking that separate good testers from truly great ones. It’s about standing shoulder-to-shoulder with the brightest QA minds, solving problems under pressure, and emerging with undeniable proof of your team’s strength and innovation.

What’s at stake goes far beyond prize money—it’s about recognition, visibility among top industry leaders, and the credibility that elevates your place in the QA community. Because in the end, the greatest reward is the glory, the spotlight, and the right to say with pride: “We outsmarted, out-tested, and outshone the rest.”

Contest Details

Registration

Register Now!

You and your team will have 3 hours to showcase creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. The contest features two distinct tracks:

  • Track A explores the possibilities of Generative AI in testing.
  • Track B puts the spotlight on conventional automation skills.


Both tracks are built around the same underlying context, ensuring fairness and comparable complexity. The problems are designed so that your strength as a testing team and as a thinking tester shines through.

A few things to keep in mind:

  • Your team must choose a track within the first 20 minutes of the start of the contest.
  • Whichever track you pick, you’ll still have a chance to advance to Round 2.
    Your choice won’t limit your journey, your solution will.
  • The winner(s) can emerge from either track or even from one single track.
    It all depends on the quality of the solutions provided.


Quick reminders:

  • Teams of 5 engineers.
  • Duration: 3 hours.
  • Any language / tool / framework of your choice.
  • All required input files and instructions will be provided at the start of the challenge.
  • Evaluation is based on correctness, adherence, clarity, and reproducibility.
